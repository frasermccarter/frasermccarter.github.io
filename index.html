5E3 Deluxe Reverb

When designing this amp, I was determined to make something really special. Not just in terms of performance, but in terms of aesthetics and craftsmanship. This amp is it. Sporting a 6v6 push-pull 17W output and a full valve-driven reverb, this amp is ideal for both home and studio use. Its solid wood cabinet, I believe, to be a huge improvement over the industry standard of a tolex wrap.

Each amp is hand-wired and assembled here in North Berwick, Scotland. Price: £1850.

Circuit Design

The circuit for this amp has been hand-drawn by me and draws from Fender’s tweed era. It is a natural evolution of arguably Fender’s best tweed amp, the 5E3 tweed deluxe. I have altered the circuit to make it more suitable for the vast range of genres that have emerged since the tweed deluxe’s inception, tightening its response while still keeping the tweed charm. It is all valve, featuring a 5Y3GT rectifier, 12AY7 and 12AX7 pres, and a pair of 6V6 output valves.

The reverb design is based around Fender’s early reverb unit, the 6G15, integrating many of its controls, unique to it, such as the dwell and tone, seldom found on more modern valve reverb amps. I’m a firm believer in not overcomplicating things for the sake of it, as such preamp controls are kept to the basics, volume and tone.

The amp also takes cues from modern solid-state circuits, introducing fail-safe diodes in the rectification stage, so there is no need to worry if it goes mid-gig.

Cabinet Design

I’m a big fan of mid-century modern design, specifically the Bauhaus philosophy of form follows function. Using the knowledge and understanding I gained during my time at the Chippendale School of Furniture, I have designed an amp that can sit as a showpiece in its own right. The cabinet is made from solid utile and is handcrafted by me here in North Berwick, Scotland. Below are some photos of the design and construction process.

Specification Sheet

  • Controls: Volume, Tone, Dwell, Reverb Tone, Reverb Mix
  • Inputs: 2x 1/4" jack sockets HI LO
  • Rectification: 5Y3GT valve with solid-state backup
  • Output: 2x 6V6 output valves in a push-pull configuration
  • Preamp: 1x 12AY7 & 1x 12AX7 valves for the input and gain stages
  • Speaker: 12" Jensen C12R speaker
  • Speaker Outputs: 1x 8ohm, 1x External Cab Jack Output
  • Onboard Effects: 9" reverb tank driven by a 12AT7 valve
