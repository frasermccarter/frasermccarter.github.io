5e3 Deluxe Reverb

This valve amp sports a push-pull 17W 6v6 output stage with a 12" Jensen C12R speaker. Inspired by Fender's 1960s circuit-design philosophy, it delivers a warm, rich tone that's perfect for blues, rock, and jazz.

Each amp is hand-wired and assembled here in North Berwick, Scotland.

Inspired by mid-century modern design, the cabanite is made from solid utile, featuring a unique front baffle design that could easily have come from the Bauhaus movement. This amplifier isn't just a tool for professional use, but a work of art that can sit proudly in any home or studio.

Spec Sheet

  • All valve design featuring 2x 6v6 push-pull output, 12ax7 preamp tubes, and a 12ay7 reverb driver.
  • 17W output power at 8 ohms.
  • 12" Jensen C12R speaker.
  • Hand-wired point-to-point construction on a turret board.
  • Custom hand-crafted ulite cabanite cabinet.
  • A 9" reverb chamber.
