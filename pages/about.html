Hey...I'm Fraser

I'm in my early 20s and I build custom guitar valve amplifiers. Valve amps started as a hobby for me, or maybe more a curiosity; I first built a simple 10W Champ in my garden shed and my interest only grew from there. Now I'm building amplifiers with custom designed circuits and a focus on finish too, taking inspiration from mid-century modern design.

As a guitarist, I've always been fascinated with the unique value valve amps have in the world of guitar tone and sound. With so many options nowadays for solid state amps, whether simulated or not, valve amps still hold a special place in the hearts of many musicians and I've fallen down the rabbit hole of trying to figure out why this is.

My goal with McCarter Amps is to create high quality, handcrafted amplifiers that not only sound great but also look great. I want to bring a fresh perspective to the world of guitar amplifiers and offer something unique for musicians who are looking for something special.
