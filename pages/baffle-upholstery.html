Custom Baffle Upholstery

I also offer custom baffle upholstery for vintage and modern amplifiers. Unlike amp repair, baffle upholstery is not limited to pre-1990s valve amplifiers. I strive to maintain as much originality and character original to the amplifier as possible.

Shoot me an email at info@mccarteramps.com and we can discuss what you're after! If possible include a photo of the amp you would like upholstered and we can discuss fabric options, whether that be something I have in stock or of your choosing.

Policy and Information

  • A £25 deposit (per amp) is taken upon drop off, this goes towards the cost of materials and labour in the end. The deposit will be invoiced via email, and will be due upon drop off. This is non-refundable.
  • Upholstery rate is £25/hour + materials. Typically a full baffle takes 1.5-2.0 hours to complete. Material cost is generally in the range of £20-£40 depending on the baffle size and fabric choice. Minimum charge is £75.
  • Turn around time is typically 1-2 weeks, this may vary depending on the complexity of the work, difficulty sourcing materials, and current workload. Please keep in mind I am only one person and I treat each amp as if it were my own.
  • Drop off and collection is by appointment only and requires a minimum of 24 hours' notice.
  • Upon completion, you will be contacted to arrange collection. Once ready it must be collected within 10 days. If this is not possible, please let me know!
  • Payment is due upon collection, I accept cash and bank transfer.
  • I warranty all my work for 90 days from the date of collection.
