Amplifier Repair

I offer amplifier repair and diagnostic services for pre-1990s valve amplifiers. I strive to maintain as much originality and character original to the amplifier as possible in my repairs.

Shoot me an email at info@mccarteramps.com and we can discuss any issues you're experiencing with your amplifier. If possible include a photo of the amp and any details about the problem you're experiencing.

Policy and Information

  • A £100 deposit (per amp) is taken upon drop off, this goes towards the cost of replacement parts and labour in the end. The deposit will be invoiced via email, and will be due upon drop off. This is non-refundable.
  • Repair rate is £50/hour + replacement parts. Typically a full service takes 2.0-4.0 hours to complete. Parts cost is generally in the range of £40-£80 depending on the issue at hand. Minimum charge is per repair is £100.
  • Turn around time is typically 2-3 weeks, this may vary depending on the complexity of the work, difficulty sourcing materials, and current workload. Please keep in mind I am only one person and I treat each amp as if it were my own.
  • Drop off and collection is by appointment only and requires a minimum of 24 hours' notice.
  • Upon completion, you will be contacted to arrange collection. Once ready it must be collected within 10 days. If this is not possible, please let me know!
  • Payment is due upon collection, I accept cash and bank transfer.
  • I warranty all my work for 90 days from the date of collection.
