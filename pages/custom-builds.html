Custom Builds / Modifications

I'm also happy to offer custom builds and modifications for existing amplifiers. Whether you're looking to upgrade your current setup or desire a very specific sound, I can help bring your vision to life.

From simple modifications like adding a solid state redundancies or changing speaker configurations, to full custom builds with unique circuit designs and cabinet aesthetics, I'm here to help you achieve the tone and look you want.

For more information or to discuss a custom build, please contact me via email or Instagram.

  • I recommend single-ended designs for their simplicity and warm tone. This could be something like a Fender 5F1 or Gibson Skylark Ga-5.
  • I am hesitant to offer anything beyond a 5e3 design due to the complexity of multi-tube designs and the time required to build them.
  • Custom builds and modifications are priced on a case-by-case basis depending on the complexity of the work and materials required. Please reach out to discuss your project and I can provide a quote.

Modifications may include:

  • Adding a solid state redundancy to an existing valve rectifier amplifier for improved reliability and reduced maintenance.
  • Changing speaker configurations, such as upgrading to a higher quality speaker or adding additional speakers for increased volume and tonal options.
  • Custom circuit designs to achieve specific tonal characteristics or features, such as adjusting the filter stage or modifying the gain stage.
  • Adjusting component values to fine-tune the tone and performance of an existing amplifier, this could for example involve changing resistor values or capacitor values.
  • Custom cabinet designs and finishes to create a unique aesthetic that matches your style and preferences.
